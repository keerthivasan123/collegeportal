Student Activities

Industrial Visits

One of the most important responsibilities of an educational institution is bridging the gap between the industry and the students. The college facilitates this transition with the help of Industrial visits. Each year, the students are made to visit an industry or company that is related to their field of study. This helps them to get an idea about the actual scenario in industries and how engineers working in the industry apply their knowledge to solve various problems. These industrial visits encourage the students to step out of their comfort zone and start honing their skills so that they can be applied to real-world problems. The students can interact with the professionals and clarify any doubts that they have. They can get a clear and unbiased opinion about the industry from them and keep it in mind while making decisions in the future. This activity helps the students to benefit from the experience of the professionals and it provides insight into their chosen career path.

NGO Activities

Even the smallest act of kindness is greater than the grandest intention. The NGO visits organized by the college pave the way for the students to become responsible members of the society. On one Saturday each year, the students visit various Non-Governmental Organizations such as orphanages and old-age homes and spend time with the residents there. The experience broadens the studentsâ€™ views about the world. The students spend time in the institution and may conduct small events and games if they desire. They share a meal with the residents and can also distribute small gifts or other necessary items to the residents to remember them by. They have a fun time with the residents and this makes the students more socially conscious. The act of giving is mainly about making a difference. With such activities, the students are encouraged to think that they too are an important member of society and that they can have a huge positive impact on the world if they wish to do so. They can understand their role in this world better and their duties as an individual and a citizen of the country.
