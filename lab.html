T10kT PROJECT- IIT BOMBAY

In 2019, St. Joseph's College of Engineering (RC ID-1581) was recognized as IIT Bombay's Remote Center for hosting IIT Bombay's online workshops under "The T10kT project" funded by the NMEICT National Education Mission through ICT. It helps faculty members to constantly upgrade themselves to learn about advancements in their field and to understand best practices to facilitate the best possible learning and to benefit from each of the programs. The use of online and unified approach allows learners to complete a significant portion of online training, thus reducing the amount of time that must be spent on face-to-face synchronous interaction.
